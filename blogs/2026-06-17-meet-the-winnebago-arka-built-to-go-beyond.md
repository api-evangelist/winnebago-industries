Winnebago introduced the ARKA, an off-road adventure truck built to reach remote destinations while keeping comfort for extended trips, evolving the brand's Backcountry Adventure portfolio. It features a four-pillar mounting system letting the house articulate independently from the truck cab, multi-zone climate control, Winnebago Connect technology, a vacuum-infused one-piece roof, and off-grid power, joining the Revel and EKKO in the adventure lineup.
