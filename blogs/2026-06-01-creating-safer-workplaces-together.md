Winnebago Industries operates a safety center of excellence (COE) focused on preventing serious injuries and fatalities across its enterprise. The COE develops standardized safety frameworks tailored to each business unit's specific risks, combining OSHA requirements with industry best practices, while emphasizing collaborative learning and employee engagement in workplace safety.
